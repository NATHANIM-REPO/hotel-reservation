Welcome to Sheraton Addis, a Luxury Collection Hotel, Addis Ababa

Find luxury and serenity in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia

Located in the heart of the Ethiopian capital, Sheraton Addis, a Luxury Collection Hotel, Addis Ababa sits on a hilltop overlooking the city, safely nestled between the National Palace and the prime minister’s residence. Our 5-star hotel in Ethiopia offers stunning views, acres of open space and a location just 20 minutes from Bole International Airport. Mixing African refinement with modern accents, the unique design of Sheraton Addis stands as the epitome of gracious living. Boasting glittering pools with enchanting underwater music, an indulgent spa menu of soothing massages at Aqva Club and several gourmet restaurants where our guests dine and socialize, our hotel delights the senses. Around-the-clock service and luxury amenities await in 295 rooms and suites designed with exceptional comfort and personalized attention in mind. For additional privacy, our secluded, three-story villas provide opulent retreats with personal outdoor pools. Find serenity at Sheraton Addis in Ethiopia.

Food And Beverages

worldly cuisine

Providing a bountiful array of flavors and aromas, the Sheraton Addis offers a choice of eleven restaurant and lounge options that will seduce the most discerning palate with culinary delights from all around the world—from Italy’s Stagioni and India’s Shaheen, to Asian and Middle Eastern nights at Summerfields.

Summerfields

An elegant space sets the stage for a special celebration featuring impeccable service and unrivaled cuisine from a variety of specialty evenings of Continental nights including Asian and Middle Eastern. Summerfields also hosts a generous Sunday Brunch buffet with unlimited sparkling wine offered to its guests.

Stagioni Itlay Restaurant

Traditional and innovative cast-iron cuisine from Italy Graced with breathtaking views, our traditional Italian restaurant provides a warm welcome to the guests with its earthed walls, wooden beams, and terracotta floor tiles.

Stanley's Bar

The first and only cocktail lounge in Addis Ababa Warm, wood paneling creates a tranquil mood, and pictorial images of Ethiopia's past transport you back in time, as you enjoy a wide range of exclusive drinks and cocktails. Exquisite selection of Martini cocktails.
