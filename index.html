Welcome to Sheraton Addis, a Luxury Collection Hotel, Addis Ababa

Find luxury and serenity in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia

Located in the heart of the Ethiopian capital, Sheraton Addis, a Luxury Collection Hotel, Addis Ababa sits on a hilltop overlooking the city, safely nestled between the National Palace and the prime minister’s residence. Our 5-star hotel in Ethiopia offers stunning views, acres of open space and a location just 20 minutes from Bole International Airport. Mixing African refinement with modern accents, the unique design of Sheraton Addis stands as the epitome of gracious living. Boasting glittering pools with enchanting underwater music, an indulgent spa menu of soothing massages at Aqva Club and several gourmet restaurants where our guests dine and socialize, our hotel delights the senses. Around-the-clock service and luxury amenities await in 295 rooms and suites designed with exceptional comfort and personalized attention in mind. For additional privacy, our secluded, three-story villas provide opulent retreats with personal outdoor pools. Find serenity at Sheraton Addis in Ethiopia.
